Quick Data inspection¶

We have 4 drillholes DH7, DH8, DH13 and DH14. From old to new they are DH7 -> DH8 -> DH13 -> DH14.

The units in the Model, (from young to old) are:

  • Quaternary
  • Andesite
  • Rhyolite (with dykes of Qtz_Porphyryie)
  • Qz-Rhyolite
  • Dacite (partly heavily altered)
  • Rhyodacite

Note, that the last entry in the boreholes is the End of the drillhole, NOT necessarily the layer-transition!
